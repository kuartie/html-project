BROKEN HEARTED?

I know you’re feeling broken right now, like the pieces of your heart or spirit are scattered and hard to put back together.
It’s okay to feel this way; it’s a testament to how deeply you’ve lived, loved, and cared.
, life can be incredibly tough, leaving us feeling shattered in ways we never expected.
But remember, brokenness doesn’t mean you’re beyond repair. In fact, it’s in these moments of vulnerability that true healing begins.
Just like a piece of pottery mended with gold, your scars are part of your story, adding depth and beauty to who you are.
They don’t diminish your worth—they enhance it.Take your time to heal, piece by piece. It’s okay to be tender with yourself, to allow yourself the space to mend.
You don’t have to rush the process. Know that each day, you are gathering strength, even if it doesn’t feel like it. And one day, you’ll look back and see just how far you’ve come.
You are resilient, and even in your brokenness, there is hope. The light will find its way back into your life, and you’ll emerge stronger, wiser, and more compassionate than before.
Keep believing in your ability to heal, and know that you are not alone on this journey.

"You are whole, even in your broken places."
